Vendors

From the creative to the cutting edge, our vendors provide us with the products consumers know and demand. North Fork Sales can help elevate your brand and open the door to unprecedented earnings potential.

Allstar Marketing Group

With over 50 original products, Allstar has been a dominant force in the field since 2002. Experts in brand management, their now ubiquitous Snuggie has enjoyed sales surpassing $500 million.

Telebrands

Industry veterans with over 30 years’ experience, Telebrands has launched hundreds of successful campaigns including the Hurricane Mop, Pocket Hose, and Stone Wave.

Northern Response

The largest distributor of infomercials in Canada, Northern Response distributes product to over 90 countries. With full market coverage in the US, their clients include major retailers such as Target, Walmart, and Bed Bath & Beyond.

Blue Banana Group

Hampton Direct

Marketers of some of the most successful products in As Seen On TV history, Hampton Direct focuses on providing real solutions to everyday household problems.

Ontel

Since 1994, Ontel has been a leader in development of innovative consumer products such as Pillow Pets and the Slap Chop. Sold in over 30 countries, their products accomplish their company mission of making their customers’ lives more enjoyable.
